How to make Amogus Salad

Amogus salad is a fun family friendly recipe for salad that has been enjoyed by European cuisine enthusiasts for hundreds of years. Read more to delve into what makes a salad Amogus.

What you need:

  • Sussy berries
  • Sussy lettuce
  • Drip dill pickles

Instructions

  1. Gather the sussy berries in a bowl and mush them
  2. Add the sussy lettuce
  3. Chop the drip dill pickles into slices and place them in bowl
  4. Enjoy!
