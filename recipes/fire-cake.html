How to make Fire Cake

Fire cake is a delicious kind of cake made of the most fire ingredients. Please keep reading so this webpage can teach you how to make firecake.

Ingredients:

  • Batter
  • Sugar
  • Sprinkles
  • Gasoline
  • Isopropyl Alchohol
  • Matches

Directions

  1. Get a large cake tray
  2. Place the batter and sugar in the tray and mix
  3. Pour gasoline, matches, and the Isopropyl alchohol in a blender and mix
  4. Pour blender solution in the tray and bake for 420 minutes
  5. Take out of oven and chow down!
